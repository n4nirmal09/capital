Find your magic number

How much you should be putting away each month depends on a few things:

Small gifts can lead to a big future

CollegeAmerica allows family members, friends and even the beneficiary contribute to the account. While grandparents may opt to make Education part of their legacy, birthdays and baby showers are also a great way for others to shape the future of someone they care about

1st Birthdays can raise an average of $5000 in contributions without gift-tax consequences.

Grandparents can contribute up to $14,000 a year towards their grandkids education

Family and friends can also collaborate by giving meaningful gifts for your child’s milestones.
